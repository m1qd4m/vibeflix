# Privacy Policy for VibeFlix

**Effective Date:** May 2026

VibeFlix ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by VibeFlix.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:
*   **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and name (managed securely via Google Firebase Authentication).
*   **Usage Data:** We collect data regarding your movie preferences and watch history to improve the AI recommendations provided to you.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect in the following ways:
*   To personalize your experience and deliver custom AI-powered movie recommendations (using Google Gemini API).
*   To manage your account and provide customer support.
*   To maintain the security and integrity of our application.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:
*   **Google Firebase:** Used for secure user authentication and database storage.
*   **TMDB (The Movie Database):** Used to fetch movie posters, trailers, and details.
*   **Google Gemini API:** Used to process your preferences and generate AI movie suggestions.

## 4. Security
We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All user data is securely stored and transmitted using industry-standard encryption provided by Firebase.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. 

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the app.
